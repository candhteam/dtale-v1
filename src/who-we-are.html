Two Decades Of Design Excellence

Established in 2000, DTALE DECOR Pvt. Ltd. is one of India’s foremost design-led, interior fit-out brand, with over 10 lakh SFT of luxury interior spaces executed for high-end hospitality and residential spaces. Our roster of clients include hospitality majors like The Sheraton, Novotel, Dunes, Samroha, Saptha Hospitality, Holiday Group, Aangan Retreat, Gokulam Hotels & Resorts and Corporates, high profile individuals and celebrities

Our folio of contract assignments also includes high-end contract grade furniture involving over 10,000 unique pieces using over 200,000 CFT in wood and work with special materials like stainless steel, wrought iron, bronze, leather, wicker, and jute. Our philosophy of design craft, inspires both passion and attention for detail across scale and span of our engagements, from integrated projects worth 25 crores to single artifact worth 1 lakh rupees.

Factory Infrastructure

Extensive manufacturing infrastructure and trained human resources lay the foundation for the delivery of high-quality products. Our manufacturing infrastructure consists of mechanized production facilities at the Edayar industrial area and a centralized warehouse at Marathakara, Thrissur with spread over an area of 1,10,000 sq. ft., covering end-to-end processes with solid Wood-working machinery, Panel processing machinery, Metalwork set-up, Upholstery unit, Finishing chamber, and Seasoning Plant. The manufacturing unit is run with state-of-the-art German and Italian Technology and with over 300 fully trained artisans.
